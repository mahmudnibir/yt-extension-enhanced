Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Built for power users who want precise control without complexity.

Speed Control

Dial playback from 0.25× to 20× with a precision slider. Fine-tune with keyboard shortcuts on any page.

Universal Video Overlay

Hover any video on any website to reveal a minimal speed control bar — not just YouTube.

Voice Modes

Five real-time audio DSP modes — Normal, Chipmunk, Bass Boost, Robot, Echo — using the Web Audio API.

Bookmarks

Timestamp any moment in a video with a label. Cloud-synced across all your Chrome sessions.

Ad Skip

Automatically detects and skips YouTube ads so you stay in flow without lifting a finger.

Content Filters

Hide videos by keyword, channel name, or duration threshold — keep your feed clean.

Watch Statistics

Track total watch time, session history, and productivity broken down by day or week.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Fully customizable hotkeys for every control. Record your own key combos directly in the popup.

Universal Overlay

Installation

Up in four steps.

No Chrome Web Store needed — load it directly from source.

Step 01

Clone or Download

Grab the repo from GitHub:
git clone https://github.com/mahmudnibir/yt-extension-enhanced

Step 02

Open Extensions

In Chrome, navigate to chrome://extensions and enable Developer mode (top-right toggle).

Step 03

Load Unpacked

Click Load unpacked and select the cloned project folder.

Step 04

Pin & Go

Pin the extension icon to your toolbar. Open YouTube and click the icon to start.
